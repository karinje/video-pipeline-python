# SunVue Aviator Classic - 30 Second Instagram Ad Concept

**SCENE 1**: Urban professional blends into the crowd during morning commute, forgettable in the sea of gray suits.

**SCENE 2**: Golden hour light hits the city as they pause outside office building, catching their reflection in glass.

**SCENE 3**: They slide on SunVue Aviator Classics and the world shifts—their posture straightens, chin lifts with quiet confidence.

**SCENE 4**: Walking down the street, heads turn as the frames catch sunset light, transforming anonymous professional into timeless icon.

**SCENE 5**: Close-up on the Aviators reflecting city skyline as they smile slightly—"SunVue. Never Hide."

---

**KEY VISUAL MOMENTS:**
- Transformation happens the instant frames slide on (peak "instant upgrade")
- Golden hour lighting makes the Aviators glow and become the focal point
- Urban professional aesthetic throughout (suits, city architecture, sleek styling)
- Confidence transformation shown through body language before/after
- Frames clearly visible in scenes 3, 4, and 5 as the style hero
