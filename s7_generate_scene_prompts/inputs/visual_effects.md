# VISUAL EFFECTS LIBRARY FOR EYEWEAR ADVERTISING

## HERO EYEWEAR EFFECTS (Perfect for showcasing frames)

**Eyes In**  
Moves the camera close to the subject's eyes, creating an intense and emotional connection with the viewer through the lenses

**Luminous Gaze**  
The subject's eyes glow with an otherworldly brilliance through the frames, piercing through the darkness. Enigmatic, radiant, and intense

**Glow Trace**  
The frames leave behind glowing trails of movement—stylized and energetic, perfect for showing frame movement

**Hand Transition**  
A hand adjusts the glasses, sweeping across the lens to pull the subject into the next moment. Personal, tactile, and engaging

**3D Rotation**  
The frames spin smoothly in place, showing a full 360° view. Clean, centered, and perfect for showcasing frame design and details

**Diamond**  
The frames transform into sparkling, faceted diamond—hard, reflective, and radiant. Perfect for luxury eyewear positioning

**Innerlight**  
A radiant glow builds from within the lenses, shining through. Feels powerful or magical—perfect for smart glasses or transformations

## TRANSFORMATION EFFECTS (Putting on glasses moments)

**Freezing**  
Time freezes as glasses go on—breath becomes visible, motion stops. Perfect for showing the moment everything changes

**Morphskin**  
The subject's face subtly shifts as frames go on—skin smooths, confidence emerges, their persona transforms to match the frame style

**Duplicate**  
The subject splits into multiple versions, each wearing different frame styles. Perfect for showing variety or collection

**Clone Explosion**  
The subject instantly splits into multiple identical versions wearing the same frames. Bold effect for showing universal appeal

**Multiverse**  
Worlds collide as subject tries different frames—each pair shows a different lifestyle/personality. Expansive and mind-bending

**Roll Transition**  
The scene spins forward as frames slide into place, smoothly transitioning from bare face to styled. Clean and dynamic

**Melt Transition**  
The old blurry world liquefies and drips away as glasses go on, revealing crystal clarity. Surreal and hypnotic
